A Question
in Frames

February 2026
I’ve spent months collecting moments — glances, silences, laughter in low light — trying to understand what this feeling really is.

And now the reel has reached the moment I can’t edit around anymore.
You already know most of the footage: late drives with no destination, the way you tilt your head when you’re thinking, how your voice changes when it’s just us.

What you might not know is how many times I’ve rewatched those scenes and still feel my chest tighten the same way.

So I’m asking — not lightly —
Will you be my girlfriend?
